# InfraRadarAI Platform Vision

InfraRadarAI is a global infrastructure intelligence platform for teams that need to discover, qualify, monitor and act on infrastructure opportunities before they become obvious in traditional databases or static market reports.

## Primary users

- **Business development teams**: find new project opportunities, track tender movement, monitor competitor awards and prioritize outreach.
- **EPC contractors and construction firms**: identify projects entering tender or award stages, watch owner and funder signals, and detect supply-chain or dispute risks.
- **Project managers**: track project status, milestones, stakeholders, risks, changes and evidence in one operational view.
- **Infrastructure consultants and advisors**: build country, sector and project briefs using verified sources, portfolio context and AI summaries.
- **Developers and sponsors**: benchmark comparable projects, monitor financing and regulatory movement, and manage early opportunity pipelines.
- **Investors, lenders and project finance teams**: monitor bankable projects, financing gaps, counterparty changes, policy shifts and risk events.
- **DFIs, MDBs and public-sector teams**: observe pipeline activity across regions, sectors and funding programs.
- **Researchers and analysts**: verify AI-discovered projects, maintain evidence quality and publish structured intelligence.

## Product promise

InfraRadarAI should feel like a command center for infrastructure markets:

1. **Discover** projects from global public sources, MDB signals, tenders, awards and market intelligence.
2. **Verify** each project with source URLs, evidence records, confidence scoring and human review.
3. **Prioritize** opportunities using sector, region, value, stage, risk and portfolio fit.
4. **Monitor** changes through agents, alerts, project updates and watchlists.
5. **Explain** decisions through AI search, portfolio chat and intelligence summaries.
6. **Act** through contact discovery, exports, reports and team workflows.

## MVP principle

The MVP focuses on delivering reliable core value without depending on fragile external AI quotas. Agent reasoning, extraction and research synthesis should use **Lovable AI** by default. External tools such as Perplexity, OpenAI, Firecrawl or specialist datasets can be added later as optional enrichment layers after customer usage validates where they improve accuracy or coverage.

## Implemented core platform

The codebase already contains the core platform surface:

- Project database with filters, detail pages and evidence.
- Global map and public exploration views.
- Natural-language project search.
- Portfolio/watchlist and notes.
- Portfolio chat.
- Alert center and saved alert rules.
- Tender and award intelligence.
- Country intelligence.
- AI market report builder and intelligence summaries.
- Human-in-the-loop review queue.
- Agent monitoring and agent task tracking.
- Contact discovery and stakeholder intelligence.
- Authentication, role-based access, billing, card-free trial, onboarding and feedback.

## Future direction

Future features should deepen the workflows already present rather than create disconnected modules:

- Win-probability and project scoring.
- Weekly email digest delivery.
- Saved search notifications.
- Enterprise API and webhooks.
- Team workspaces and shared portfolios.
- Dedicated tender events schema.
- Contact database page.
- Scheduled market reports.
- Optional external research connectors for premium tiers.
