## Application Answers

### What are you working on?

InfraRadar Pulse - an AI-native platform that replaces $200,000/year infrastructure intelligence reports with real-time AI agents at $199/month.

We aggregate real-time project data from 5 multilateral development banks (World Bank, IFC, ADB, AfDB, EBRD), run 40+ AI agents to score and monitor each project, and deliver personalized alerts, research, and intelligence to infrastructure investors, EPC contractors, and project finance teams.

---

### What problem are you solving?

Infrastructure investors, EPC contractors, and project finance banks need real-time intelligence on trillions of dollars in globally active infrastructure projects. The current market is dominated by incumbents (MEED, GlobalData, Wood Mackenzie) charging $3,000-$200,000/year for quarterly PDF reports written by human analysts.

These tools are slow, static, generic, and priced out of reach for most market participants. Yet the underlying data - published by the World Bank, IFC, ADB, AfDB, and EBRD - is completely public. Incumbents hire analysts to read it. We deploy AI agents.
