### Describe what your company does in detail

Infrastructure BD teams, EPC contractors, project finance desks, infrastructure PE funds and advisory firms need real-time intelligence on trillions of dollars in globally planned and active infrastructure. Today they pay $3K–$200K/year to MEED, GlobalData, Wood Mackenzie, IJGlobal and regional publishers for quarterly PDF reports written by 50–100-person analyst teams. The underlying data is public.

InfraRadarAI is the AI-native replacement. 30+ specialised agents continuously ingest 7 MDB pipelines — World Bank, IFC, ADB, AfDB, EBRD, AIIB and IADB — plus 20+ procurement portals, regulatory feeds, contractor filings and tender awards. Every project carries a source URL and confidence score. Unverified records are capped at 30% confidence and routed through a human review queue with mandatory verification reasons.

### What do you understand about your business that other companies in it just don't get?

1. **The data is public; the moat is verification.** Every MDB publishes project data freely. The defensible product is the verified-intelligence layer on top — source URLs, confidence scoring, human review queue, audit trail. Incumbents can't rebuild this without destroying margin; new AI entrants skip it and ship hallucinations.

2. **Cross-MDB is the actual product.** No single incumbent aggregates across all 7 MDBs we cover. WB-only watchers miss AfDB co-financing; ADB trackers miss IFC equity in the same country. The cross-MDB co-financing graph is structurally hard for incumbents to replicate.

3. **Buyers want to query, not download.** DFI staff and EPC BD managers are sophisticated professionals. The interaction model incumbents sell (quarterly PDF) is wrong, not just expensive. Plain-English Ask + Portfolio Chat + AI Report Builder is what they actually want.

4. **Lovable-AI cost structure flips unit economics.** Our agent stack runs at near-zero marginal cost. The unit economics that justified MEED's 2015 analyst floor don't survive the Gemini 3 / GPT-5 era.
