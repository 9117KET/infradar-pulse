# InfraRadar Pulse - Letter of Intent Target Tracker

> Track all LOI outreach. An LOI for accelerator purposes is 1-3 sentences of expressed intent - not a binding commitment. Most contacts can sign with zero legal friction.
> Last updated: April 2026

---

## What to Ask For

For accelerator applications (YC, a16z, Techstars), you need:
- A **paragraph email or letter** from a named professional expressing intent to use or trial InfraRadar
- Ideally on company letterhead or a professional email signature
- No legal review required - this is an expression of interest, not a contract

Minimum viable LOI text:
> "I am [Name], [Role] at [Organization]. I have reviewed InfraRadar Pulse and intend to trial the platform for [use case - e.g., MDB project pipeline monitoring, tender intelligence, deal sourcing]. I would use it for [specific workflow]. I found it compelling because [one reason]. I look forward to participating in the early access program."

## Outreach Message (LinkedIn / Cold Email)

**Subject line:** Early access - infrastructure intelligence platform ([specific MDB they care about])

**Message body (adapt per persona):**
```
Hi [Name],

I noticed you [reference specific work - TTL on [Project], spoke at [Conference], manage [Region/Fund]].

I'm building InfraRadar - a platform that aggregates real-time intelligence from the World Bank, IFC, ADB, AfDB, and EBRD into one AI-scored dashboard. It replaces the $3k-$200k/year incumbent platforms at $199/month.

Given your work on [specific domain], I think it could cut [specific workflow] significantly.

I'm not selling anything yet - we're in early access and I'm validating with a small group of domain experts. Would a 20-minute call make sense?

[Your name]
```

**Do not** pitch the LOI directly in the first message. Earn the conversation first.
